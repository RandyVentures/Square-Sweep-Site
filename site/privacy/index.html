Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Square Sweep handles information when you use the Square Sweep mobile app and related support pages.

Who We Are

Square Sweep is operated by Randy Torres. Questions about this policy can be sent to randyventures06@gmail.com.

Data Stored on Your Device

Square Sweep stores gameplay preferences locally on your device, including best score, last score, selected theme, sound preference, haptics preference, preferred mode, onboarding completion, purchase flags, and review prompt counters.

This local data is used only to run the app experience and is not sold.

Purchases

Square Sweep uses RevenueCat to support in-app purchases, restore purchases, and unlock purchase entitlements such as Remove Ads and Premium Pack. RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, transaction status, app user identifiers created by the SDK, and related diagnostics needed to validate purchases.

Advertising

Square Sweep uses Google AdMob for optional rewarded ads only. Gameplay does not include banner ads. Rewarded ads are requested as non-personalized ads, and Square Sweep does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

AdMob may process device, ad delivery, consent, and diagnostic information needed to serve and measure ads according to Google policies.

Sharing

The Share Score feature creates share text on your device. Sharing happens through the system share sheet or clipboard action you choose.

Data Deletion

You can remove local Square Sweep data by deleting the app from your device. Purchase restoration data is handled through Apple and RevenueCat purchase systems.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as Square Sweep evolves. Material changes should be reflected in the app release notes or on this page.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, email randyventures06@gmail.com.
