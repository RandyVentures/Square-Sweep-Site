Support

Help for Square Sweep, a calm 8x8 block puzzle game for iPhone.

How do I play?

Place the three available pieces on the board. Full rows and columns clear automatically. New pieces appear after all three current pieces are used.

What is Classic mode?

Classic mode tracks score, combos, best score, undo, restart, and game over detection.

What is Zen mode?

Zen mode keeps the same puzzle rules with calmer visuals and softer scoring.

What is Daily Sweep?

Daily Sweep gives you a deterministic seeded puzzle run for the day. It is local to the app and does not require an account.

How do purchases work?

Remove Ads removes optional ad prompts. Premium Pack unlocks Neon, Wood, and Ocean themes. Purchases and restore purchases are handled by Apple and RevenueCat.

Are there banner ads?

No. Square Sweep does not show banner ads during gameplay. Rewarded ads are optional.

The app is not working correctly. What should I try?

  • Close and reopen Square Sweep.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • Confirm you are running the latest available iOS version.
  • Update Square Sweep from the App Store when an update is available.
